Lakmal Buddika Meegahapola

Doctoral Student at EPFL, Research Assistant at Idiap Research Institute

I'm a PhD student at École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) and a Research Assistant in Social Computing Group at Idiap Research Institute advised by Prof. Daniel Gatica-Perez. I'm interested and experienced in using Data Science and Analytics, Machine Learning for research in Ubiquitous & Mobile Computing. Currenty, I am part of the large scale EU-funded project "WeNet - The Internet of US" and my research revolves around "Diversity-Aware Machine Learning for Rich Mobile Sensing Data".

Before starting my PhD, I worked as a Research Engineer at Ubiquitous & Mobile Computing Group in Singapore Management University, Singapore under the supervision of Prof. Archan Misra. I got my BSc Engineering degree (Computer Science and Engineering) from University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka.

My work has been recognized at prestigious events such as Microsoft Imagine Cup World Finals, Asia Pacific ICT Alliance, and National Best Quality ICT Awards (Sri Lanka) and has been covered by local and international press. Further, I was part of the Sri Lankan International Olympiad team for Astrophysics.
